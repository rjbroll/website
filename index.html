I am a fourth-year PhD candidate in Economics at Princeton University. My research interests are in econometrics and labor.

Before I began my studies at Princeton, I was a predoctoral research fellow at the Stanford Institute for Economic Policy Research (SIEPR) working with Professor Heidi Williams. I received my BA in Economics, Mathematics, and Mathematical Methods in the Social Sciences at Northwestern University in 2020.
